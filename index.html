Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

St. Xavier's School features a cherished church built in 1978, which has since become a cornerstone of the school’s spiritual life. The church, with its elegant architecture and serene atmosphere, provides a tranquil sanctuary for students and staff alike. Its stained glass windows, depicting scenes from the life of St. Xavier, bathe the interior in a kaleidoscope of colors, creating a contemplative and inspiring environment. Over the years, the church has hosted countless significant events, including weekly Masses, special ceremonies, and community gatherings, fostering a deep sense of unity and tradition within the school. The church's presence on campus underscores the importance of faith and community in the holistic education that St. Xavier's School strives to provide.



school library

Was built in 1980

St. Xavier's School is home to a distinguished library established in 1980, which has since become a hub of knowledge and learning for its students and staff. Nestled at the heart of the campus, the library's architecture reflects a blend of classic and modern styles, creating an inviting and conducive atmosphere for study and research. With its extensive collection of books, periodicals, and digital resources, the library supports a wide range of academic pursuits and intellectual curiosities. Over the years, it has hosted numerous literary events, study groups, and educational workshops, fostering a vibrant culture of learning and inquiry. The library stands as a testament to St. Xavier's School's commitment to academic excellence and the holistic development of its students.



school auditorium

Was built in 1982

St. Xavier's School proudly features an impressive auditorium built in 1982, which has since become a central venue for the school's vibrant cultural and academic activities. Designed with excellent acoustics and spacious seating, the auditorium offers a perfect setting for a wide range of events, from theatrical performances and music concerts to lectures and assemblies. Its state-of-the-art lighting and sound systems enhance every event, making each occasion memorable for both performers and audience members. Over the years, the auditorium has hosted countless school plays, talent shows, award ceremonies, and guest speaker events, fostering a strong sense of community and school spirit. This iconic space underscores St. Xavier's School's dedication to providing a well-rounded education that celebrates the arts, public speaking, and collective gatherings.
